#Cloud Diver

A (planned to be) Semiautonomous drone designed to sample otherwise inaccesible parts of weather systems utilizing a novel wing folding design allowing rapid dives through adverse wind conditions that would otherwise damage or destroy a conventional aircraft or drone.

Current Features: Total BOM cost of less that 200 dollars (support equipment excluded) A full length "keel" and internal flexible spars to allow flight even after heavy damage A foldable wing design built using simple components, mainly steel hinges, fishing line, and a 20kg strength servo motor A payload bay with support for temperature, humidity and barometric pressure sampling as well as a camera for visual observation. Oversized full moving combination Elevator, rudder, and aileron control surfaces for extreme control authority in all phases of flight A modular design built around individual sections fitted together on 2mm steel rods.

In Progress features: A bleed air system to allow even greater takeoff with wings extended and flight control with wings retracted Additional modules, including a mechanism to allow launch from other flight platforms

Planned Features: Deformable wings to allow greater control authority Autonoumous "Return to home" capability in case of signal loss Wind speed and direction monitoring, as well as percipitaion detection.
